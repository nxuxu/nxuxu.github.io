MIX

The fruit wastes are mixed and ground into fine pieces

BLEND

They are further blended with bio-binder into a mixture

FORM

The mixture then forms sheets of materials

DESIGN

The material then made into a great range of products

CYCLE

With recycle, redesign and remanufacturing, the material enters a closed-looped system, extending its lifespan and realize its full potential.

We believe that sustainable material development must be accompanied by outstanding performance, aesthetics and versatility that enables timeless design.






Peelsphere is available in a range of colours and patterns. We only use natural or Oeko-Tex certified dyes. Laser-cut and printing make Peelsphere unique and beautiful. Peelsphere can also be embroidered, woven, and sewn into end products that are biodegradable and timeless.
