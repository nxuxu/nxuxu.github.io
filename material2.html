MIX

The raw materials are mixed together and grinded into fine pieces.

BLEND

They are further blended and heated with some bio-based raw material into a fluid of matrix.

FORM

The resulting matrix can be poured into pre-shaped molds of various patterns and forming sheets of material after drying.

REACH

Material can be processed with different techniques and made into great range of products.

CYCLE

With recycle, redesign and remanufacturing, the material enters a closed-loop ecosystem, prolonging their lifespan, or bio-decomposed into new life.

We believe that sustainable material development must be coupled with outstanding performances, aesthetics, practicability and revolutionary customizability that ensures a limitless design possibility.




Peelsphere is available in two finishes, Bio-Based and Bio-Based embedded with fruit wastes. Both featuring enhanced performance and great versatility. It is a breakthrough in material science as well as bio-innovation and a platform for brands to reimagine their products and connect to their consumers.
