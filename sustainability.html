MATERIAL INNOVATION

PEELSPHERE is dedicated to material innovation and circular design. Through recycling, redesign, and reuse, it fully realises the potential of fruit waste and algae, transforming into products with aesthetics and performance. PEELSPHERE biomaterials that are 100% recyclable, providing a pioneer solution that is responsible and sustainable for the global textile industry.




EARTH REGENERATION

At PEELSPHERE, we build material like how nature does. Our technologies leverage fruit wastes and responsibly-sourced biopolymers, the final products of PEELSPHERE can be infinitely recycled in our closed-loop ecosystem or reintegrated into the natural eco-systems, building a sustainable capital for our mother earth.




SUSTAINABLE LIFE CYCLE

RAW MATERIAL


Implementing a sustainable waste management system that includes bio waste collection and re-usage, the use of biodegradable materials, and non-toxic solvents can greatly reduce the environmental impact of industrial processes. PEELSPHERE only uses plant extracted or bio-based colour pigments.

PRODUCTION


The company's commitment to an optimised clean production process has led to reduced energy and resource consumption, decreased waste generation, and promotion of sustainable industrial standards.

DESIGN


Design for circularity and versatility is part of our DNA and competitive advantage.

END OF LIFECYCLE


Optimised recycling specifications and an improved material circulation system can result in a reduced environmental impact. At the end of the life cycle, PEELSPHERE biomaterials can be recycled, remanufactured, redesigned, to give the material an infinite life loop.

PEELSPHERE biomaterials are biodegradable: it is not only environment-friendly, but it also helps to nourish new lives.






OUR IMPACT FOCUSES

BIODIVERSITY


We aim to protect and restore biodiversity. By using fruit waste and responsibly sourced raw ingredients, we are creating natural materials that can be returned to nutrient cycles as well as reduce the amount of waste that would otherwise end up in landfills or the natural environment.We are creating a more sustainable and regenerative ecosystem that can support a diverse range of species and contribute to the health of our planet.

CLIMATE CHANGE


Our material manufacturing process are engineered to eliminate the use and generation of hazardous and toxic substances as well as reduce GHG emissions. By prioritising sustainability and regeneration, PEELSPHERE is a force for positive changes and contributes to create a more thriving and resilient planet for all those who inhabit it.

CARBON FOOTPRINT


PEELSPHERE invests in energy-efficient technologies and equipment and uses renewable energy. We are also trying to reduce and optimise the transportations by implementing a local sourcing strategy, offering telecommuting options for employees.

WATER


Our entire production process is designed for water conservation, it is more water-efficient compared to traditional textile and leather industry. Additionally, there is no waste water generated in our production process.

By focusing on these impact areas, we can make significant progress towards our vision of creating a more sustainable future, while addressing the interconnected social and environmental challenges we face today.
