About PEELSPHERE™

PEELSPHERE™ is a material innovation company founded by a team of talented designers, engineers, and entrepreneurs passionate about the sustainability and future of our planet. We come together for a common goal of solving the material waste problem by engineering the future generation of biomaterials. Through advanced material engineering, we incorporate aesthetics and design principles to challenge the status quo of sustainability.
